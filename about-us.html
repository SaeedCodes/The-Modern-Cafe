What is our business?

We are a company that has a goal to create a good, flavourful and enjoyable coffee. The reason that this is our goal for our business is because we realized that many coffee enjoyers never get the proper experience of a coffee that they deserve.

Our History

Our company was founded in 2010 in order to pursue our dream of making a cafe that many people would enjoy. Present-day, we have successfully reached that dream of owning a cafe shop that people will enjoy.
